About Me

After a decade of working in customer service, Jennifer Yowell is no stranger to hard work, dedication, problem solving and effective communication. As a Facility Manager of a metropolitan community center, Jennifer utilized and developed computer systems to solve problems and meet the needs of internal and external stakeholders. Jennifer has a Masters Degree from The University of Tennessee and a Bachelors Degree from George Mason University. Jennifer grew up in Virginia and is an outdoor enthusiast enjoying kayaking, golf, and hiking. In the Spring of 2015, she recently completed an emersion program in Web & Front End Development at Tech Talent South.
